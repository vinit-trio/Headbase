UX/UI Designer

Location / Job Type

On-site • Full Time

About the Role

Do you love to think outside the box and deeply consider UI interactions for every element? We are searching for UI/UX designers who are eager to take on the challenge of creating an Outstanding Design Project. We are prepared to elevate your creativity.

What do we look for

Key Responsibilities

  • Create high-fidelity, responsive mockups with visual clarity and consistency
  • Lead or support 2-3 junior UI designers through feedback, planning, and task reviews
  • Structure layout and information architecture: sitemaps, page flows, and navigation logic
  • Build scalable design systems using Auto Layout, Variants, Tokens
  • Organize Figma files: clear layers, grouped elements, interaction documentation
  • Communicate design intent clearly to developers, PMs, and international clients

Core Requirements

  • 3-5+ years of UI design experience (web, mobile, SaaS)
  • Strong portfolio showing visual craft, structure, and dev-handoff qualit
  • Expert-level Figma usage (Components, Tokens, Prototyping)
  • Comfortable in client calls, design reviews, and cross-team syncs
  • Experience building sitemaps, layout hierarchies, and design frameworks
  • Ability to manage junior team members with clarity and empathy

Benefit

  • Two weekly holidays.
  • Learning budget
  • Yearly salary review.
  • Two festival bonuses.
  • Paid leaves
  • A creative and collaborative work environment.
  • Team hangout, participate in sports events
  • Health insurance benefit after completion 1 year.
