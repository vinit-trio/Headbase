Ellitee SkinCare

Elitee Skincare is a premium skincare brand focused on science-backed formulations for acne-prone and sensitive skin. The objective of this project was to design and develop a high-performance e-commerce platform that reflects brand credibility.

Service

Web Design & Development

Industry

Skincare / Beauty

Year

2025

Project Requirement

The client approached us with clear business and digital goals:

  • Establish a strong online presence for their skincare brand
  • Create a trustworthy and professional brand image
  • Improve mobile shopping experience
  • Increase product discovery and conversions
  • Simplify the checkout and ordering process
  • Build a scalable platform for future growth

Problem and Solution

Problems

  • No existing digital presence
  • Low brand trust
  • Unclear product hierarchy
  • Requirement for a fast, mobile-first, and scalable.
  • Limited scalability

Solutions

  • Built scalable platform
  • Designed trust-focused UI
  • Structured product pages
  • Mobile-first architecture
  • Optimized performance

Typography & Color

We used the Poppins typeface for its modern, clean, and highly readable style across all digital interfaces. Bold headings and balanced body text create strong visual hierarchy and easy readability. The Soft Coral, Canvas White, and Jet Black color palette reinforces clarity, trust, and a premium brand identity.
